Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 12, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Game Site ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you visit our website.

2. Information We Collect

Our games run entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. However, our website may use:

  • Local Storage: Some games may save your high scores or preferences locally in your browser. This data never leaves your device.
  • Cookies: We use cookies for basic functionality and analytics.
  • Analytics Data: We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics (pages viewed, time on site, device type, location data).

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. Google Analytics collects anonymous information such as IP addresses, browser types, page views, and session duration. This data is used solely to improve our website experience. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. Learn more at Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdSense: If advertisements are enabled in the future, Google AdSense may use cookies to display personalized ads based on your browsing history. You can learn more about how Google uses data at Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use cookies for:

  • Remembering your preferences
  • Analyzing site traffic and usage patterns (via Google Analytics)
  • Delivering relevant advertisements (if enabled in the future)

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect site functionality and prevent analytics tracking.

5. Data Security

Since our games run entirely in your browser and we don't collect personal data, there is minimal security risk. Any data stored locally (like high scores) remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

6. Children's Privacy

Our games are suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we don't collect personal data from anyone, this applies universally.

7. Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store your personal data directly, there's no data for us to provide, modify, or delete. However, you can:

  • Clear your browser's local storage to remove saved game data
  • Clear cookies through your browser settings
  • Opt out of Google Analytics tracking using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Opt out of personalized ads through Google Ads Settings

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party websites.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository: sanjith-esp32.github.io

11. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you are located in the European Union:

  • We do not process personal data unless you explicitly provide it
  • Third-party services (like Google AdSense) may process data - see their privacy policies
  • You have the right to data access, rectification, and erasure (though we don't store your data)
  • You can withdraw consent for cookies at any time through browser settings

12. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have specific privacy rights. Since we don't collect or sell personal information, these rights primarily apply to third-party services we use. You can:

  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Request information about data collected by third parties
  • Control cookie preferences
